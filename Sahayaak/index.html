Who are we?

We are bunch of crazy innovators, mind sharing a common passion towards technology; who wanted to be the helping hand in our own little possible way, when our country was suffering and struggling in this pandemic situation. We decided to use our strength our holding on the technology to build something, to help those who we identified needed us the most during this challenging time “the worker class” , “ the people on street”, “the hawkers” who are trying to make end meets to survive in this tough time.

Building on this thought we are presenting you “Helping Hand” an application which bridge this gap. Our mission is simple “Make the food available to the right people at the right time and at right place so no one sleeps hungry”.

As our honourable Prime Minister said “कोरोना वायरस के खिलाफ लड़ाई लंबी है, इसलिए ना थकना है और ना ही हारना है सिर्फ जीतना है”

This is our small contribution to our nation.

Features

Seeking Help?

Just select your location and place a request.

Stay updated

You can checkthe status of your requests and connect with your helping hand if requires.

Ready to help?

See requests near your, and provide whatever help you can.

Check nearby requests

You can see the people seeking requests near your location and connect with them if required.
